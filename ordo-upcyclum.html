Ordo Upcyclum

Upcycled Warhammer 40k Art E-commerce Store

Project Overview

Ordo Upcyclum is an e-commerce store specializing in upcycled Warhammer 40k art created by a hobby enthusiast who transforms discarded materials into unique framed artwork.

Project Details
  • Type: E-commerce Store
  • Industry: Art & Collectibles
  • Niche: Warhammer 40k Fan Art
  • Website: ordoupcyclum.com
Key Features
  • Product catalog with filtering by factions
  • Multiple frame type options
  • Shopping cart and checkout
  • Responsive design
  • Clean, minimalist UI

Store Concept

The store's tagline, "upcycled Warhammer art by a hobby nerd who hates waste," perfectly captures the mission: giving new life to discarded Warhammer 40k materials by transforming them into beautiful framed art pieces.

Product Offerings

The store features a variety of framed Warhammer 40k artwork, including:

  • Adepta Sororitas - Sisters of Battle themed artwork
  • Abaddon - Chaos Warmaster pieces
  • Various other Warhammer 40k factions and characters
  • Multiple size options (5 1/2" x 7 1/2", 12 3/4" x 16 3/4", etc.)
  • Different frame styles to match customer preferences

Screenshot showing the homepage with featured products including framed Warhammer 40k artwork

Technical Implementation

The website appears to be built on a modern e-commerce platform with:

  • Clean navigation with dropdown menus for Factions and Frame Types
  • Product grid layout with high-quality product photography
  • Price display in USD
  • User account functionality
  • Shopping cart integration
  • Search functionality

Target Audience

The store caters to:

  • Warhammer 40k enthusiasts and collectors
  • Tabletop gaming fans
  • Eco-conscious consumers interested in upcycled products
  • Art collectors looking for unique gaming-themed pieces

Business Model

Ordo Upcyclum operates as a direct-to-consumer e-commerce business, selling unique, handcrafted art pieces that combine sustainability with fandom. Each piece is upcycled from existing materials, making every item unique while reducing waste.
