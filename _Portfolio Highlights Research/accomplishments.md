## Featured Products

### OneBag.Travel — founder & full-stack developer (2017–present)
An independently built, live web product for one-bag / carry-on travelers. It combines a
database of carry-on backpacks with ratings and reviews, user-generated packing lists for
over a dozen countries, and an airline carry-on rules reference tool. John designed,
built, and operates the whole thing end-to-end — including a professionally designed brand
and a rebuild on a modern serverless stack.

- **Role:** Founder, designer, full-stack engineer, operator
- **Tech:** ASP.NET Core, Azure Functions, Cosmos DB
- **Live:** https://onebag.travel/
- **Why it matters:** A real, live product owned start-to-finish — the strongest proof of independent product ability.

### Movid — CTO & Co-Founder (Home Exercise Plan Builder, 2012–2014)
Co-founded a healthcare/physical-therapy software startup and served as CTO. Led
full-lifecycle development from prototype to paying customers of the **Home Exercise
Plan (HEP) Builder** (app.movidhep.com) — a SaaS tool that helps physiotherapists
prescribe patients high-definition video home-exercise plans. As the sole developer,
John worked on everything from UI design to the NoSQL back end, with an Azure + GitHub
continuous-delivery pipeline and a performant graph search that wasn't practical on a
relational database. Featured for startup/website feedback (StartUpLift) and recognized
at startup events.

- **Role:** CTO & Co-Founder (sole developer)
- **Tech:** ASP.NET MVC, AngularJS, Knockout, RavenDB (NoSQL)
- **Why it matters:** Founder-level ownership, a real product with paying customers, and startup recognition.
